Our Story

Turning a Vision into Reality

Our journey started with a simple belief—freelancing can be more than just an alternative career path; it can be a thriving, sustainable profession.

  • 2022

    I took a leap of faith, connecting skilled professionals with my clients. They loved the results, and I realized the potential of building a network of talented freelancers.

  • 2024

    The dream started taking shape. Seeing the growing demand for reliable virtual assistants, I envisioned a platform that would empower both businesses and freelancers.

  • 2025

    Virtual Bridge Company was born! Our mission is to bridge businesses with exceptional Filipino talent, providing quality, efficiency, and affordability.

Meet the Founder

Jennesis Adriano (Jena)

Founder | Finance Professional | Entrepreneur

With years of experience in financial services, I’ve worked with global companies, handling bookkeeping, financial analysis, and strategic planning. From my early days as a General Accountant at Accenture PH to becoming a Financial Controller in the UK’s Esports/Gaming sector, I’ve developed a strong passion for efficiency and business growth. Now, I’m turning that passion into a business—helping fellow freelancers find meaningful opportunities while providing businesses with top-tier virtual assistants. When I’m not working, I love traveling, meeting new people, and finding joy in organizing and planning.
